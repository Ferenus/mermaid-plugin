# Privacy Policy — Mermaid Diagrams for Confluence

**Last updated:** April 7, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the "Mermaid Diagrams for Confluence" Forge app handles data. This app is open-source and distributed under the MIT License.

## What Data We Collect

This app collects and processes only the minimum data necessary to provide its functionality:

- **Diagram content** — the Mermaid diagram code you create or edit.
- **Version history metadata** — when a diagram is saved, the app records the diagram code, a timestamp, and the display name of the Confluence user who performed the save.

No additional personal data is collected. The app does not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any form of behavioral monitoring.

## How Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively within **Atlassian Forge Key-Value Storage (KVS)**, which is part of the Atlassian cloud infrastructure. Diagram data never leaves your Atlassian instance and is never transmitted to external servers.

The app makes **no external API calls** of any kind. It does not communicate with any server or service outside of the Atlassian platform.

## Data Sharing

This app does **not** share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. There are no external services, advertising networks, or analytics providers involved.

## Data Retention

- Diagram content is retained in Forge KVS for as long as the corresponding Confluence macro exists on a page.
- Version history is limited to a maximum of **50 entries per diagram**. When this limit is reached, the oldest entries are automatically removed.
- Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from Forge storage in accordance with Atlassian's Forge data retention policies.

## Your Rights

Because all data resides within your Atlassian instance and is governed by your organization's Atlassian account, you can:

- View and edit any diagram content directly in Confluence.
- Delete diagrams by removing the macro from a Confluence page.
- Uninstall the app to remove all stored data.

For any data-related requests, contact your Atlassian organization administrator or reach out using the contact information below.
